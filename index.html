Our philosophy

At Loved Pets Animal Rescue, we believe all animals deserve a chance to find a loving forever home. We provide initial veterinary care to rescued animals, then work to match each animal with a human family to love them and keep them safe forever. All animals are screened for diseases upon arrival, washed and groomed, and then added to our website for adoptive families to see. Potential adopters are screened for adoption history, household lifestyle, and long-term commitment to care.

We can always use volunteers! Volunteers help bathe & groom animals, socialize pets, clean, and assist with administrative tasks. If you’re interested, follow the steps in our volunteer section.

How to become a volunteer

  1. Complete the online application (save it as volunteer-form.pdf in your folder).
  2. Submit the application by emailing it to volunteer@welovepets.com.
  3. Participate in a brief phone interview.
  4. Attend a volunteer training session on the first Saturday of every month.

We're grateful for every hour you can give — volunteers make everything possible.
