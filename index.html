Our story

Paws & Home began in 2015 when our founder noticed how many pets were overlooked at traditional shelters. We're different—we take time to understand both animals and adopters, creating matches that last.

Our cozy shelter feels more like a home, where animals get personalized care while they wait for their forever families. We don't believe in cages or time limits—just patience and proper matches.

All our pets are spayed/neutered, vaccinated, and microchipped before adoption. We also offer post-adoption support because every transition needs a helping hand.

Can't adopt? You can still help

Even small donations provide food, medical care, and cozy bedding for animals in need. We're a 501(c)(3) nonprofit—every dollar makes a difference.
